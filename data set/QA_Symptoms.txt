1 Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)
- Symptoms:                                        									
  - feel often nervous
  - have trouble concentrating
  - have trouble in sleeping

2 Panic Disorder
- Symptoms:
  - feel often panic
  - sudden bursts of rapid breathing
  - sudden bursts of excessive sweating


3 Major Depressive Disorder (Depression)
- Symptoms:
  - feel hopeless
  - often find yourself feeling negative
  - always tired
  - changed eating pattern
  - get suicidal thoughts


4 Social Anxiety Disorder
- Symptoms:
  - avoid people or activities
  - an introvert
  - addicted to social media


5 Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
- Symptoms:
  - get pop ups of a stressful memory
  - frequently experience nightmares


6 Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
- Symptoms:
  - trouble focusing on a task


7 Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD)
- Symptoms:
  - feel angry
  - overreact
  - blame yourself for an unfortunate event


8 Schizophrenia
- Symptoms:
  - get hallucinations


9 Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
- Symptoms:
  - exhibit repetitive behavior


10 Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD)
- Symptoms:
  - seasonal depression?


11 Bipolar Disorder
- Symptoms:
  - experienced an increase in energy?

->Additional Overlaps
- Major Depressive Disorder (Depression) and GAD: Both can involve feelings of hopelessness, trouble concentrating, and sleep disturbances.
- Panic Disorder and PTSD: Both can include sudden bursts of panic and physical symptoms like rapid breathing and sweating.
- Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD) can sometimes overlap with Major Depressive Disorder in terms of emotional instability and feelings of worthlessness.
